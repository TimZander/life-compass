# Day 2 — Values

**Time:** ~65 min · **Goal:** name what you stand for.

Values are **not aspirations.** They're the principles you actually use to evaluate
whether a life feels right. Today you'll narrow a long list down to 5 ranked,
operationalized values.

---

## 1. Brainstorm from the list (15 min)

Open the [values starter list](../reference/values-list.md) and circle (or copy below)
every word that feels like **you** — not who you wish you were, who you actually are at
your best. Add your own.

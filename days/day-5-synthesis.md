# Day 5 — Synthesis & Life Compass

**Time:** ~80 min · **Goal:** build the artifact, then test it against your actual life.

Today you assemble the compass and pressure-test it against the five dimensions of your
life. The compass is useless if it doesn't change what you do.

---

## 1. Assemble your compass (20 min)

Open [`templates/life-compass.md`](../templates/life-compass.md) and fill it in using
your work from Days 2–4: values (ranked, with meanings), passion themes, purpose
statement, and non-negotiables.

> Tip: copy the template to `my-compass.md` at the repo root so the blank template stays
> clean for future annual redos.

- [ ] Values filled in
- [ ] Passions filled in
- [ ] Purpose filled in
- [ ] Non-negotiables filled in

## 2. Test against the five dimensions (40 min)

For each dimension ask: **Is my current setup aligned with the compass? Where's the
gap?**

### Career

- Does my work let me live my values daily, or require me to suppress them? ______
- Does it use my passions, or just my skills? ______
- Is it serving my purpose, or unrelated to it? ______
- **One specific change that would move this closer to aligned:** ______

### Money

- Am I earning and spending in service of what I value, or of habits/expectations? ______
- What am I overspending on that doesn't serve the compass? ______
- What am I underspending on that would? ______
- **One change:** ______

### Place

- Does where I live support my values, passions, and purpose? ______
- What would have to be true about where I live for it to be a full yes? ______
- **One change:** ______

### People

- Who in my life amplifies the compass? Who pulls against it? ______
- Who do I need more of? Less of? ______
- **One change:** ______

### Time

- Look at last week's calendar. What % of waking hours served the compass? ______
- What recurring time commitments don't? ______
- **One change:** ______
